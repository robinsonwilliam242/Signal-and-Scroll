## Cheap content makes attention expensive

When synthetic content floods feeds, the signal-to-noise ratio collapses.

## The new scarcity

Trust. Provenance. Human taste.

## What might win

Smaller communities. Strong editorial filters. Creators with a track record. And platforms that treat verification as a feature, not an afterthought.
