## Parasocial is a human default

People bond. Stories bond us. The issue is when bonding becomes a lever.

## Where it gets toxic

Creators are incentivized to offer intimacy for engagement. Brands buy that intimacy. Platforms sell the attention.

## What changes it

Transparency. Boundaries. And business models that don’t require constant closeness.
