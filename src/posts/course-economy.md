## The new credential is a checkout page

Courses are everywhere because they’re scalable, high-margin, and sell hope. Some are great. Many are not.

## The dividing line

A good course gives you a **transferable skill** and proof. A bad course gives you vibes, urgency, and a funnel.

## What to look for

Clear outcomes. Transparent pricing. Real examples. Refund policies. And a creator who can explain the thing without a montage.
